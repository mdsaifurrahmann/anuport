my story

I often find myself reflecting on the intersection of diverse skills and passions, and how they shape unique career paths. My journey bridges a love for content creation and marketing with a technical foundation in software engineering, and recently, a deep passion for UX/UI design.

Rather than viewing these fields as separate, I see them as complementary.
My technical expertise allows me to approach design with a problem-solving mindset, while my creative background inspires
